BOOKDU Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Your financial data stays on your device. BOOKDU is designed with privacy first - all your job records, payment details, and expense information are stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server.

Overview

BOOKDU is a payment tracking app for freelancers. We believe your financial data is personal and should remain under your control. This privacy policy explains how BOOKDU handles your information.

Data Storage

All data you enter into BOOKDU is stored locally on your device:

  • Job and payment records - Stored in a local database on your iPhone
  • Expense records - Stored locally on your device
  • Tax calculations - Computed locally based on your entered data
  • App preferences - Stored locally on your device

We do not operate any servers or cloud services. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g., CSV export for your accountant).

Camera and Photo Library Access

BOOKDU requests access to your camera and photo library for one purpose only:

  • Payslip OCR scanning - To photograph or select images of payslips for automatic data extraction

Images you capture or select are:

  • Processed locally on your device using on-device OCR
  • Stored locally as attachments to your job records
  • Never uploaded to any external server

Data We Do NOT Collect

BOOKDU does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Your financial records, exact payment amounts, or payslip images
  • Any data that could identify you personally

No Account Required

BOOKDU does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. There is no sign-up, login, or registration process.

Analytics

BOOKDU uses PostHog, a privacy-focused analytics service, to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This analytics data is:

  • Anonymous - No personal identifiers, names, emails, or device IDs are collected
  • Aggregated - We see general usage patterns, not individual user behavior
  • Limited - Only basic app events like "app opened" or "job created" are tracked
  • Privacy-safe - Financial amounts use ranges (e.g., "$100-500") rather than exact values

This data helps us understand which features are most useful and identify areas for improvement. It is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.

No Advertising or Tracking

BOOKDU does not integrate with any advertising networks or cross-app tracking services. We do not use advertising identifiers or track you across other apps or websites.

Data Export

You can export your data as a CSV file at any time. This export is initiated by you and the file is saved to your device or shared via iOS sharing options. We have no access to exported files.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting individual jobs or expenses removes them from your device
  • Uninstalling BOOKDU removes all app data from your device
  • There is no cloud data to request deletion of

Children's Privacy

BOOKDU is a financial tracking tool intended for adult freelancers and is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
